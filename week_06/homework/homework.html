Police Shootings Data Exploration

Number of Police Shootings by Age Group

I chose this histogram to represent the age groups involved in police shootings. The histogram allows us to clearly see that individuals between the ages of 20-30 were the most involved in shootings. Further information that breaks down this age group by gender or race would be helpful in this visualization.

Number of Police Shootings by Race

I chose this bar chart to represent the races involved in police shootings. The bar chart allows us to clearly see which races were the most involved in shooting incidents (white and black in this data). Further demographic information that highlights more about the people within the respective races could be useful.

Number of Police Shootings by Gender

I chose this pie chart to break down the police shootings by gender. The pie chart allows us to clearly see which gender is the most involved in shootings (male in this data) (the chart was also strengthened by only having three categories which helped make the point even clearer). Again, having additional demographic information and specific differences between the genders could be useuful supplementary information.
